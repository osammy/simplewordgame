RULES ON HOW TO PLAY THE GAME
This is a simple word game. You are to form words from the words on the tiles, the number of words contained in each field is stated to the right side of the board. Each completed word attracts 5 points after all words are completed from stage 1 you are automatically moved to stage 2. Words are filled from top to bottom. Enjoy!
